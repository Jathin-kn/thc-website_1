01 Free consultation & initial assessment +

We connect you with the right specialist at no cost. Medical history review, symptom discussion, preliminary diagnosis, and honest explanation of risks, benefits, and alternatives.

02 Pre-surgical evaluation +

Blood tests, imaging (MRI, CT, X-ray), cardiac fitness (ECG, 2D Echo), and anesthesiologist assessment. We guide you through every test so nothing delays your surgery.

03 Honest cost estimate +

Personalized surgical plan with a full written cost breakdown — procedure, stay, medications. Package options discussed. You know the number before you sign anything.

04 Insurance & documentation +

Verification of coverage, pre-authorization from your insurer or TPA, and cashless hospitalization facilitation wherever eligible. All the paperwork — handled.

05 Pre-operative preparation +

Admission coordination, fasting and medication instructions, consent forms reviewed — we make sure nothing falls through the cracks the night before surgery.

06 Surgery day support +

Your surgeon and team take over inside the OT. We stay on call for the family outside — real-time updates wherever possible, so someone is always reachable with answers.

07 Post-operative in-hospital care +

Recovery monitoring, wound care, pain management coordination, and daily doctor round tracking. We flag anything that needs attention before it becomes a problem.

08 Discharge & insurance claim +

Final bill review, discharge summary, claim submission and follow-up. We handle the financial closure so the last memory of this experience isn't a pile of forms.

09 Post-discharge support +

Follow-up consultations, stitch removal, physiotherapy guidance, and emergency contact. Because care doesn't end at the hospital gate.
