How we walk
with you

From the moment you reach out to long after you're home — here's exactly what we do.

1
First contact

Free consultation & initial assessment

We connect you with the right specialist at no cost. You share what's happening — the symptoms, the fear, the uncertainty. We listen, and find you the right doctor for your condition.

  • Medical history review and symptom discussion
  • Preliminary diagnosis and surgical recommendation
  • Explanation of risks, benefits, and alternatives
2
Before surgery

Pre-surgical evaluation

Blood tests, imaging, cardiac clearance — we guide you through every test that's needed so nothing delays your surgery and you walk into the OT fully prepared.

  • Blood tests, MRI, CT scan, X-ray as required
  • Cardiac fitness evaluation (ECG, 2D Echo if needed)
  • Anesthesiologist assessment and fitness clearance
3
Transparency

Honest cost estimate

Before anything is scheduled, you receive a clear written estimate. No vague answers, no surprises on discharge day. You know exactly what you're walking into before you commit.

  • Personalized surgical plan from your surgeon
  • Complete cost breakdown — procedure, stay, medications
  • Package options discussed wherever available
4
Admin support

Insurance & documentation

We verify your coverage, file for pre-authorization, and facilitate cashless hospitalization wherever possible. All the paperwork — handled.

  • Verification of insurance coverage and benefits
  • Pre-authorization from your insurer / TPA
  • Cashless hospitalization facilitation if eligible
5
Day before

Pre-operative preparation

Admission, fasting instructions, consent forms, medication adjustments — we make sure nothing falls through the cracks the day before surgery.

  • Admission process guidance and room coordination
  • Pre-surgery fasting and medication instructions
  • Consent forms reviewed and signed
6
Surgery day

Surgery day support

Your surgeon and their team take over inside the OT. We stay on call for the family outside — so someone is always reachable when you have questions or need reassurance.

  • Coordinated handover to surgical team
  • Continuous on-call support for waiting family
  • Real-time updates wherever possible
7
In hospital

Post-operative in-hospital care

Recovery monitoring, wound care, pain management, daily doctor rounds — we track progress and flag anything that needs attention during your hospital stay.

  • Recovery room monitoring immediately after surgery
  • Wound care, dressing, and pain management coordination
  • Regular doctor rounds and progress tracking
8
Going home

Discharge & insurance claim

Final bill, discharge summary, claim submission — we handle the financial closure so the last memory of this experience isn't a stack of forms to deal with alone.

  • Final bill preparation and review
  • Discharge summary including diagnosis and recovery instructions
  • Insurance claim submission and follow-up
9
After discharge

Post-discharge support

Follow-up consultations, stitch removal, physiotherapy guidance, and emergency contact — because care doesn't end at the hospital gate.

  • Follow-up consultations (often included or discounted)
  • Stitch removal and wound checks coordinated
  • Rehabilitation and physiotherapy guidance where needed
  • Emergency contact support for any post-surgery concerns
